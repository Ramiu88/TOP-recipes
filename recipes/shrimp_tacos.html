Shrimp Tacos Recipe

Description

The shrimp is saut√©ed in honey butter, topped with a delicious mango salsa, and wrapped in a tortilla. It tastes worth a million bucks yet is so simple. Everyone will be begging for more.

Ingredients

  • 1 mango -- peeled, pitted, and diced
  • 1 ripe avocado - peeled, pitted, and diced
  • 2 tomatoes, diced
  • 1/2 cup chopeed fresh cilantro
  • 1/4 cup chopped red onion
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons lime juice
  • 1/4 cup honey butter
  • 1 pound salad shrimp
  • 4 (10 inch) flour tortillas, warmed

Steps

  1. Toss mango, avocado, tomatoes, cilantro, onion, garlic, salt, and lime juice together in a bowl. Cover and refrigerate for 30 minutes.
  2. Melt honey butter in a skillet over medium-high heat. Add shrimp; cook and stir until pink and opaque, 2 to 3 minutes.
  3. To serve, place a few shrimp onto a warm tortilla, top with mango salsa, and folf up .Repeat with remaining ingredients
